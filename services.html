Move Management

Revolution's move management coordinators provide move management solutions that alleviate and minimize disruption to your business. As a result, your employees are able to focus on getting settled into their new surroundings and getting back to work.

It's more than just moving someone from here to there. It's about efficiency, accountability, finding the right suppliers, ensuring satisfaction every step of the way, and saving time and money. We see the big picture.

Benefits of business move management

Business move management is the integration of every component necessary to exit one space and enter another or support ongoing occupancy. This type of strategic consultation comes with tremendous benefits.

  • Single Point of Accountability
  • Strategic Support
  • Informed Business Decisions
  • Streamlined Communication
  • Consistency
  • Transparency
  • Reduced Risk
  • Cost Savings
Move Coordinator responsibilities
  • Assist client with developing the move schedule
  • Maintain and manage move schedule
  • Maintain and manage move spreadsheet
  • Attend construction and move meetings
  • Coordinate with department move coordinator and end users
  • Address any special needs from employees
  • Coordinate with Mover
  • Coordinate with IT Department
  • Identify printer locations
  • Walk thru space before move (punch list)
  • Follow up after move (Command Center)
